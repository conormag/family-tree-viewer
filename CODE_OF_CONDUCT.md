# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

**Encouraged:**
- Constructive feedback and helpful code review
- Welcoming newcomers and answering questions patiently
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissive or discouraging responses to contributions
- Any conduct that would make someone feel unwelcome

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
